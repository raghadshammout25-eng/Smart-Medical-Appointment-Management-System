Cardiology

Disorders of the heart and blood vessels, focuses on diagnosing, treating, and preventing conditions such as heart dise

We provide comprehensive heart care including ECG testing, blood pressure monitoring, stress tests, and personalized treatment plans to help you maintain a healthy heart and prevent cardiovascular complications.

Neurology

focused on diagnosing and treating disorders of the nervous system, including the brain, spinal cord, and nerves.

We offer expert neurological evaluations, EEG testing, migraine management, and personalized care plans to help you maintain optimal brain and nerve health with confidence and clarity

Hepatology

study, diagnosis, and treatment of liver, gallbladder, pancreas, and bile duct disorders.

We offer advanced liver function testing, hepatitis screening, ultrasound diagnostics, and personalized treatment plans to support your liver health and ensure early detection and effective management of liver-related conditions.

Pediatrics

health and medical care of infants, children, and adolescents. It covers everything from routine checkups and vaccinations

We provide comprehensive pediatric care including growth monitoring, immunizations, developmental assessments, and treatment for common childhood conditions—all in a warm, family-friendly environment.

Eye care

diagnosis and treatment of eye conditions. It includes routine eye exams, vision correction, and management of diseases

We offer comprehensive eye exams, prescription glasses and contact lenses, vision screenings, and early detection of eye diseases to help you see clearly and protect your long-term eye health.
